PaedScale · MVP

Dosing children where the guidelines run out.

Adult PK → pediatric starting dose via allometry × organ maturation, with cited rationale. Decision support, not a prescription.

Under 2 yr — maturation needs postmenstrual age.
Blank = assume term (40 wk). Preterm without this over-estimates the dose.
Enter a case and calculate. The agent will show its reasoning, then a graded, cited recommendation.
PaedScale is a research prototype for a hackathon. Output is a defensible starting estimate for a qualified clinician, not an autonomous order, and not a validated clinical-decision-support device. Narrow-therapeutic-index drugs require TDM.
